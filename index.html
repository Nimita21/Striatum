BIO

Hailing from the gritty streets of Chicago, Illinois, Striatum is a powerhouse metal band that seamlessly blends the raw intensity of death metal, the breakneck speed of thrash metal, and the haunting atmosphere of doom metal. Formed in the depths of the city's underground scene, Striatum has carved a name for themselves with their ferocious live performances and uncompromising musical vision. At the heart of Striatum's sonic onslaught is Connor Wilson, a force to be reckoned with on bass and vocals. Wilson's guttural growls and thunderous basslines form the backbone of the band's sound, driving each song with relentless aggression and emotion. Andrew Georgiev and Chris Black unleash a whirlwind of razor-sharp riffs and blistering solos on guitars, creating a wall of sound that assaults the senses and leaves audiences in awe of their technical prowess and musicality. Completing the lineup is Christian Carson, whose thunderous drumming provides the perfect rhythmic foundation for Striatum's complex compositions and dynamic shifts. Carson's precision and power behind the kit elevate the band's live shows to a visceral experience that leaves fans craving more. With influences ranging from classic metal legends to modern extreme metal innovators, Stiatum's music is a potent blend of aggression, melody, and darkness. Having conquered the local scene with their explosive performances, Striatum is poised to spread their message of sonic devastation far and wide. With a growing fan base hungry for more, this Chicago-based metal machine shows no signs of slowing down as they continue to push the boundaries of extreme music. Brace yourselves for the descent into madness with Striatum!
